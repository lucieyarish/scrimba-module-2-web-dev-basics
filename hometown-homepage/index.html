Top three activities to do in Brno

Enjoy the view of the so-called Petrov

The rooftop of Tržnice Brno at Zelný trh is the perfect spot to view the Cathedral of St. Peter and Paul from.

Visit functionalist Villa Tugendhat

Learn about the history of Villa Tugendhat built in 1929–1930.

Explore Veveří Castle and the Brno Lake

Castle Veveří, one of the largest castle grounds in the Czech Republic, was created gradually during its almost 800-year history.
